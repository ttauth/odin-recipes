Alfredo Pasta

Description

Alfredo pasta is a classic Italian dish known for its creamy and indulgent flavors. This delectable creation features perfectly cooked pasta tossed in a velvety sauce made from rich Parmesan cheese, butter, and cream. The pasta is coated in the luscious sauce, creating a harmonious blend of flavors and a luxurious texture. With each bite, you'll experience the creamy richness of the sauce combined with the tender pasta. Alfredo pasta is often garnished with freshly ground black pepper and chopped parsley, adding a touch of freshness and visual appeal. This dish is a true delight for pasta enthusiasts seeking a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces (225 grams) of fettuccine pasta (or any pasta of your choice)
  • 1/2 cup (113 grams) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (240 ml) heavy cream
  • 1 cup (100 grams) grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the fettuccine pasta and cook it according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  2. Prepare the sauce: In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Pour in the heavy cream and stir well to combine. Cook the mixture for a few minutes until it begins to simmer.
  3. Add Parmesan cheese: Gradually sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese into the skillet while stirring continuously. Continue stirring until the cheese is fully melted and the sauce becomes thick and creamy.
  4. Season and combine: Season the sauce with salt and pepper according to your taste preferences. Add the cooked fettuccine pasta to the skillet and toss it in the sauce until it is evenly coated.
  5. Serve: Transfer the Alfredo pasta to serving plates or bowls. Garnish with chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness. Serve hot and enjoy!
